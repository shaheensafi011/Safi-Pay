What is the Problem?

Over 40 million Afghans, including 8+ million living abroad, lack access to reliable international banking.

  • No access to international cards (Visa/Mastercard)
  • Inability to receive global payments
  • Dependence on informal money transfer systems

The SafiPay Solution

SafiPay is a multi-currency digital banking platform built for Afghans worldwide.

Multi-Currency Accounts

Open a digital account in USD, EUR, and GBP.

Visa Cards

Access virtual and physical Visa cards for global purchases.

Easy Financial Management

Manage all finances through a modern mobile app.
